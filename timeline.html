Timeline of the Zodiac Killer's Crimes

Dec 20, 1968

First Victims: Betty Lou Jensen & David Faraday

Betty Lou Jensen and David Faraday were shot and killed on a remote road near Vallejo, California. The Zodiac Killer later took credit for their murders in later letters.

July 4, 1969

Second Victims: Darlene Ferrin & Michael Mageau

Darlene Ferrin and Michael Mageau were shot while parked at a lover’s lane. Michael Mageau survived the attack and gave a description of the killer.

July 31, 1969

First Zodiac Letter to the Press

The Zodiac Killer sent his first letter to the San Francisco Chronicle, containing cryptograms and taunts directed at police. The letter included a cipher that he demanded be printed in the newspaper.

Sept 27, 1969

Third Victims: Bryan Hartnell & Cecelia Ann Shepard

The Zodiac attacked Bryan Hartnell and Cecelia Ann Shepard near Lake Berryessa. The killer approached them while they were sunbathing, tying them up and then stabbing them. He carved a symbol into Hartnell’s car door.

Oct 13, 1969

Second Zodiac Letter

The Zodiac Killer sent another letter to the San Francisco Chronicle, this time including a cryptogram with more letters and more claims of responsibility for the murders.

Oct 11, 1969

Fourth Victim: Paul Stine

Paul Stine was a cab driver who was shot and killed in San Francisco. This was the last confirmed Zodiac murder. Police later received cryptic phone calls from the killer, claiming responsibility.

March 13, 1971

Third Zodiac Letter

The Zodiac Killer sent a third letter to the San Francisco Chronicle, which included more threats and cryptograms. It also contained details about his previous crimes.

Unsolved

Unsolved Victim: Cheri Jo Bates

Cheri Jo Bates was murdered in Riverside, CA. Though the Zodiac Killer later claimed responsibility, it remains unsolved whether he was the actual killer.

1974-1975

Possible Zodiac Activity Continues

There were a series of letters that continued in the mid-1970s, with the Zodiac maintaining his claim of responsibility for various crimes.

1978

Final Known Zodiac Letter

The last known letter from the Zodiac Killer was received in 1978, though some later letters are suspected to be from the killer, their authenticity remains debated.
