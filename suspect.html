Key Suspects in the Zodiac Killer Case

Arthur Leigh Allen

A schoolteacher and convicted sex offender, Allen was a primary suspect. He was linked by evidence like a Zodiac-brand watch.

Richard Gaikowski

A journalist who matched descriptions and was identified by a surviving victim's voice recognition.

Ross Sullivan

A student with cryptology knowledge and connections to Riverside's Cheri Jo Bates case.

Lawrence Kane

A suspect identified by handwriting analysis and ties to the Zodiac's activity zones.

Richard Marshall

Marshall fit many Zodiac profiles, including habits and statements made in letters.
